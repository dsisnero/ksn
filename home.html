LEARN MORE ABOUT KSN
    
The FAA's Knowledge Services Network (KSN) is an adaptable, integrated, shared community environment where individuals can manage content and processes, improve business insight, simplify internal/external collaboration and empower users to make a strategic impact. Click KSN for more information!
 
 
 
TRAINING EVENTS
    Participate in an engaging experience and discover how Knowledge Management concepts are practically applied through KSN. Learn KSN Administrator and Facilitator roles and responsibilities, and take a hands-on approach understanding features and best practices.  Hands-on activities will cover lists, document libraries, user permissions, creating new sites, and much more.  Click KSN Training for more information!
